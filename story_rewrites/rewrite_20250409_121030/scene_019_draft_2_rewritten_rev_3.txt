**What Works Exceptionally Well:**

*   **Anya's Internal State:** The shift from *telling* us Anya feels connected to *showing* it through her actions, her inability to meet Kael’s gaze, and the description of her “calling” is excellent. This is a huge improvement.
*   **Leo's Investigation:** The progression of his investigation, from identifying anomalies to realizing the severity of the breach, is well-paced and believable. The “organic energy signature” is a fantastic detail.

*   **Strong Imagery:** Descriptions like "fragmented memory construct" and "cascading data streams" are vivid and evocative.
*   **Parallel Structure:** The alternating perspectives between Anya and Leo work well, building suspense and revealing different facets of the mystery.

**Suggestions for Refinement (Mostly Minor):**

*   **Kael's Role:** Kael feels a little flat. While his concern is understandable, he’s primarily functioning as a worried observer. Consider giving him a bit more agency or internal conflict. Perhaps he has a past connection to the Old World, or he's skeptical of the System and its control. This could add another layer of complexity.
*   **"Organic" Description:** While "organic energy signature" is a great phrase, consider *why* it’s organic. Is it mimicking brainwaves? Does it have a biological component? Even a small hint could add to the mystery.
*   **Lockdown Protocol – Consequences:** You state Leo initiates a lockdown protocol, but we don't *see* the immediate consequences. A brief description of the system reacting – flickering lights, distorted data, a sense of instability – would heighten the tension.
*   **Anya's Reaction to the Lockdown:** If the lockdown affects Anya within the simulation, even subtly (a momentary disorientation, a glitch in her surroundings), it would connect her experience more directly to Leo's actions.
*   **Final Sentence – Tighten:** The final sentence, “And something… was coming through,” is a bit cliché. Consider a more evocative and specific image. For example: “And a flicker of something *other* began to bloom within the data stream.”

**Specific Line Edits (Suggestions):**

*   **"Her neural pathways were subtly re-routing..."** – Consider: "Her cognitive pathways were subtly re-routing..." – "Cognitive" feels slightly more appropriate in a virtual environment.
*   **"A chill ran down Leo’s spine."** – This is a bit of a cliché. Consider replacing it with a more subtle physical reaction, like a tightening of his jaw or a prickling sensation on his skin.
*   **"A flicker of something *other* began to bloom within the data stream."** (Replacing the final sentence) - This is just a suggestion, of course!

This is a *very* strong scene. You've successfully built tension, deepened the mystery, and created compelling characters. The pacing is excellent, and the writing is evocative and engaging. The suggestions above are minor refinements, and the scene is already excellent as is.
