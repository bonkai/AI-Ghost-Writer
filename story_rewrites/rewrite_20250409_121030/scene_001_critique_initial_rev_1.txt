*   **Memory Conjuring – Weak Integration:** The scene establishes Leo can conjure things, but the planned attempt to conjure a childhood memory (the pet) is absent. This is a key plot point to establish unease and should be integrated.
*   **Show, Don't Tell – Unease:** The "nagging unease" is *told* to us repeatedly. Demonstrate this through Leo's *actions* and *physical sensations* – a twitch, a hesitation, a change in breathing – rather than stating it.
*   **Geometric Distortions – Missed Opportunity:** The plan calls for “disturbing geometric distortions.” These are alluded to with the static, but need to be *visually* described and connected to the memory attempt. This is the core seed of the story’s conflict and needs more emphasis.
