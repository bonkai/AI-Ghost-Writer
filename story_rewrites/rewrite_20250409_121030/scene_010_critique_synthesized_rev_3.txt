## Decision: Approved with Minor Revisions

This is a strong continuation of the story with compelling mystery and well-established atmosphere. The introduction of Leo and the hints of Project Chimera are excellent. 

**Minor Revisions to Consider:**

1. **Pacing/Tension:** Tighten the initial descriptive paragraphs and interweave more active investigation *between* dialogue to maintain a consistently higher level of tension.
2. **Dialogue Variety:** Reduce repetition in the Seeker’s warnings and break up Leo’s exposition with Anya’s questions/reactions.
3. **Show, Don't Tell:** Look for opportunities to *show* emotions (like Leo's desperation) through action and expression rather than stating them directly.



The core narrative is engaging and the cliffhanger is effective. These minor revisions will elevate the scene further.
