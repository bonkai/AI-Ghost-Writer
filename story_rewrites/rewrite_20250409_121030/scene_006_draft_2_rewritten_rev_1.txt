## Scene 6: Static and Recognition

**INT. LEO’S LAB - NIGHT**

The lab is a stark contrast to the world outside. Cables snake across the floor, monitors display cascading code, and disassembled tech components litter the workbenches. LEO (30s, sharp, increasingly frantic) stares intently at a large central screen displaying a violently glitching feed – Anya’s clearing.

The image fractures, dissolving into blocks of distorted color. Static washes over Anya and Kael as they stand before the Seeker, now projecting a holographic advertisement.

The ad is unsettling. Hyper-real people lounge on pristine beaches, effortlessly conjuring food and entertainment. A smooth, synthetic voice promises “SimuLife: Perfection. Simplified.” The visuals are *too* perfect, too clean.

Leo’s breath hitches. He recognizes the architecture, the impossible geometry of the towers, the precise curvature of the parks. It’s his world, rendered as a product.

**LEO**
(Muttering)
No… it can’t be.

The feed deteriorates, then momentarily stabilizes, revealing Anya’s face – confusion quickly giving way to dawning realization as she stares directly at the projection. The image collapses back into static.

**INT. CLEARING - CONTINUOUS**

Anya stares at the holographic ad, her hand reaching out, not to touch, but to *analyze* the shimmering image, tracing its edges with her gaze. Kael stands beside her, bewildered. The ad loops, showcasing effortless luxury.

**ANYA**
(Quietly)
The rendering… it’s flawless. Too flawless. The light sources don’t obey physical laws.

**KAEL**
(Frowning)
Some kind of old recording?

The ad glitches, perfect smiles twisting into grotesque distortions. Anya flinches, but her gaze remains fixed.

**INT. LEO’S LAB - CONTINUOUS**

Leo frantically types, running diagnostics. Alarms blare, red lights flash. He isolates a frame of the advertisement, magnifying a section of cityscape. He runs a cross-reference with his system architecture. The results flood his screens – a perfect match.

**LEO**
(Whispering, horrified)
The architecture… the code… it’s identical.

He zooms in on Anya’s face in the feed, enhancing the image. He sees the gears turning in her mind, the realization forming.

**LEO**
(To himself)
She's seeing it.

He slams his fist on the desk. He needs to reach her, to explain before…

**LEO**
(Desperate)
This can’t be happening.

**INT. CLEARING - CONTINUOUS**

The advertisement abruptly cuts out, replaced by static. The Seeker hums quietly. Anya doesn’t respond to Kael’s questioning gaze. She stares at the Seeker, her gaze fixed on the lens. 

**ANYA**
(Under her breath)
It wasn’t meant to inform. It was meant to *sell* something. A reality.

**INT. LEO’S LAB - CONTINUOUS**

Leo stares at the flickering feed. The connection feels fragile. He’s trapped, a prisoner in the very perfection he created. He has no idea how much time he has left.

He sees Anya’s eyes meet the Seeker’s lens, a spark of understanding passing between them.

**LEO**
(Desperate)
Anya!

The screen flickers violently, the image of Anya dissolving into static.

**FADE TO BLACK.**
