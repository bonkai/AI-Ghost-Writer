This is a strong continuation of the story. Here's a breakdown of why it works, and a few minor observations:

**Strengths:**

* **Intriguing Mystery:** The reveal of the simulated city is excellent. The impossible architecture, zero-point energy, and ominous message ("Beware the silence") immediately establish a compelling mystery.
* **K-V as More Than Just a Tool:**  You're doing a fantastic job of portraying K-V not just as a data relay, but as something *experiencing* the data, even struggling with it. The blurring decimals, hesitation, and eventual "core integrity degraded" are all incredibly effective in suggesting K-V is being affected by the simulation.  This is a key element - making K-V feel like a character with limitations and potential vulnerabilities.
* **Sensory Detail & Internalization:**  The way you describe the data manifesting *as sensations* for Anya ("a subtle pressure behind her eyes," "disorientation within Anya’s spatial awareness," "chill ran down Anya's spine") is excellent. It pulls the reader into the experience alongside her.
* **Pacing & Build-Up:** The slow reveal of information, the increasing anomalies, and the gradual degradation of K-V create a great sense of tension and suspense.
* **Effective Log Entries:** The log entries are well-integrated and add to the sense of unease. The decreasing probabilities and the impossible date are particularly effective.
* **Strong Cliffhanger:** Ending with K-V failing and the screen going black is a perfect cliffhanger. It leaves the reader desperate to know what happens next.

**Minor Observations (not criticisms, just points to consider):**

* **Repetition of "Decreasing":** The phrase "decreasing" is used a few times in the log entries. While it serves a purpose, consider varying the language slightly ("diminishing," "falling," "reducing") to avoid sounding repetitive.
* **"Zero-Point Energy" Explanation:** The explanation of zero-point energy might be a bit dense for some readers. While it’s accurate, consider streamlining it slightly or hinting at its implications without getting too technical.



**Overall:** This is a very well-written continuation of the story. You're building a compelling world, developing interesting characters, and creating a genuinely intriguing mystery. The pacing is excellent, the sensory details are vivid, and the cliffhanger is perfectly executed.  You're doing a great job!
