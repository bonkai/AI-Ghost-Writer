## Report: Passed, with Notes

This is a strong opening scene. It effectively establishes Leo's character, his world, and the initial unsettling disturbances. The writing is evocative and creates a palpable sense of unease. 

**Here's a breakdown of why it works and some minor notes:**

* **Strong Character Introduction:** Leo is immediately presented as someone obsessed with efficiency, control, and a rejection of imperfection. His dismissal of the bacon and meticulous eating habits are telling.
* **Worldbuilding:** The description of the city and Leo’s apartment paints a clear picture of a highly advanced, sterile, and controlled environment.
* **Effective Use of Sensory Detail:** The contrast between the smooth, polished surfaces of Leo's world and the grainy, chaotic image of the rural landscape is well done. The emphasis on sound (or lack thereof) is also effective.
* **Rising Tension:** The glitches and distortions escalate gradually, creating a growing sense of unease. The personal nature of the disturbances (affecting memories, causing physical tremors) is particularly intriguing.
* **Good Pacing:** The scene unfolds at a measured pace, allowing the reader to absorb the details and feel the growing tension.
* **Intriguing Mystery:** The source of the disturbances remains a mystery, leaving the reader wanting to know more.

**Minor Notes/Suggestions (mostly stylistic):**

* **Repetition of "perfect/polished":** While it reinforces the theme, the words "perfect" and "polished" are used fairly frequently. Consider varying the language slightly to avoid redundancy.
* **"Ghost in the machine" cliché:** While not a major issue, the phrase is a bit overused. You might consider finding a more original way to express the idea.
* **Tremor description:** The description of the tremor is good, but could be slightly more impactful. Perhaps focus on *how* it feels – is it painful, unsettling, or something else?
* **Final line:** The final line ("He needed to understand...") is a bit of a statement of intent. Consider ending on a more evocative image or a question that leaves the reader with a stronger sense of anticipation.



**Overall:** This is a very promising start to the story. The writing is strong, the worldbuilding is compelling, and the mystery is intriguing.  **Passed.** I'm eager to see where you take it.
