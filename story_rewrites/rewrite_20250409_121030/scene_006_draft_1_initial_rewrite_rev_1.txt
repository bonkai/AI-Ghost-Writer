## Scene 6: Static and Recognition

**INT. LEO’S LAB - NIGHT**

The lab is a stark contrast to the pristine, effortless world outside. Cables snake across the floor, monitors display cascading lines of code, and disassembled tech components litter the workbenches. LEO (30s, sharp, increasingly frantic) stares intently at a large central screen. It displays a grainy, unstable feed – Anya’s clearing, but glitching violently. 

The image flickers, fracturing into blocks of distorted color. Static washes over Anya and Kael as they stand before the Seeker, now projecting a holographic advertisement onto the clearing’s dust. 

The ad is… unsettling. Polished, hyper-real people smile vacantly, lounging on pristine beaches, effortlessly conjuring food and entertainment. A voiceover, smooth and synthetic, promises “SimuLife: Perfection. Simplified.” The visuals are *too* perfect, too clean.

Leo’s breath hitches. He recognizes the architecture, the clothing, the *everything*. It's his world. But…seen through a warped, commercial lens.

**LEO**
(Muttering)
No… it can’t be.

The feed deteriorates further, the image dissolving into a chaotic mess of pixels. Then, for a fleeting moment, the static clears, and Leo sees Anya’s face, her expression a mixture of confusion and dawning realization. She’s looking *directly* at the projection.

**INT. CLEARING - CONTINUOUS**

Anya stares at the holographic ad, her hand instinctively reaching out to touch the shimmering image. Kael stands beside her, equally bewildered. The ad loops, showcasing a life of effortless luxury.

**ANYA**
(Quietly)
What… is this?

**KAEL**
(Frowning)
Some kind of… memory? A recording?

The ad glitches, the perfect smiles momentarily twisting into grotesque distortions. Anya flinches.

**ANYA**
It feels… wrong. Like a beautiful lie.

**INT. LEO’S LAB - CONTINUOUS**

Leo is frantically typing, running diagnostics, his fingers flying across the keyboard. Alarms blare, red lights flash.

**LEO**
(To himself)
Energy spikes… system instability… the cascade is accelerating!

He zooms in on a specific frame of the advertisement, isolating a section of a cityscape. He recognizes the sleek, curving towers, the perfectly manicured parks. It’s *his* city.

He runs a cross-reference with his own system architecture, a complex web of data streams. The results flood his screens, confirming his worst fears.

**LEO**
(Whispering, horrified)
The architecture… the code… it's a match. This isn't a recording. It's… a simulation. *My* world is a simulation.

He stares at the screen, his face pale with shock. The image of Anya, frozen in a moment of confusion, burns into his mind.

**INT. CLEARING - CONTINUOUS**

The advertisement flickers again, then abruptly cuts out, replaced by static. The Seeker hums quietly. Anya shakes her head, trying to make sense of what she just saw.

**ANYA**
It felt… hollow. Like a dream you can’t quite remember.

**KAEL**
(Skeptical)
Just an old advertisement, Anya. Probably from before the Collapse.

Anya doesn’t respond. She stares at the Seeker, her gaze fixed on the lens that had projected the image. A chilling realization dawns on her.

**ANYA**
(Under her breath)
No… it felt… *constructed*. Like someone was trying to sell us something.

**INT. LEO’S LAB - CONTINUOUS**

Leo is slumped in his chair, staring blankly at the screen. The chaotic data streams continue to flow, but he barely registers them. He's lost in a vortex of disbelief and fear. 

He focuses on the feed from the clearing, and sees Anya looking directly at the Seeker. He zooms in, enhancing the image. 

He sees the confusion in her eyes, the dawning realization. And in that moment, a terrifying truth hits him. 

She knows. Or, she's about to.

He feels a surge of desperate urgency. He needs to reach her. He needs to explain. But how? He's trapped inside this digital cage, a prisoner of his own perfect world.

He slams his fist on the desk, a wave of frustration and fear washing over him.

**LEO**
(Desperate)
This can’t be happening. This *can't* be happening.

The screen flickers, the image of Anya wavering in the static. The connection feels fragile, tenuous. He has no idea how much time he has left.

**FADE TO BLACK.**
