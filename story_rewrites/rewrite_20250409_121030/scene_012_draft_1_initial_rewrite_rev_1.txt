“What *is* this System?” she asked the Seeker during one of their repair sessions.

“A complex network,” the Seeker responded, “designed to sustain…life.  Its scale is…immense.  Its function…essential.” It paused, its internal processors clearly struggling. “I am unable to provide further details.  My memory banks are…corrupted.”

Anya continued to work, driven by a growing sense of unease. People moved with an effortless grace, their faces devoid of the hardship and struggle that defined the Remnant’s existence.  Anya couldn’t explain it, but she felt a strange pull towards this world, a sense that it held the key to understanding their own past, and perhaps, their future.
