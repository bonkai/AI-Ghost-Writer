2. **Pacing - Streamline Debate:**  Tighten the extended back-and-forth. Focus on *reaction* to arguments rather than repeating points.
3. **Sharpen Ending:** Add a final, unsettling detail or image to create more anticipation and leave the reader wanting more.

*   **Worldbuilding:** Excellent atmospheric detail.
*   **Characters:** Distinct personalities, but some (Kael, Elara) need deeper motivations and more nuanced dialogue.
