## Overall Assessment: Approved with Minor Recommendations

This is a strong piece of writing with a compelling premise and effective build-up of tension. The world-building is intriguing, and the dynamic between Anya and K-V is promising. The dialogue and pacing are generally good, but could be further refined for greater impact. 

**Here's a summary of the feedback, consolidated for clarity:**

1. **Tighten Initial Pacing:** The very beginning feels a bit methodical. Focus on *immediately* establishing something is wrong, rather than a step-by-step report.
2. **Show, Don't Just Tell (K-V):**  Instead of K-V simply stating facts, *describe* what it's analyzing, letting the reader (and Anya) draw conclusions.  
3. **Build K-V's Degradation Earlier:** Subtly hint at K-V’s compromised state throughout the scene, not just at the end.



I'm eager to see where you take this story! The foundation is excellent, and with a few refinements, it has the potential to be truly captivating.
