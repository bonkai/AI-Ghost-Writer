## Overall Assessment & Decision

**Decision: Approved with Minor Revisions Recommended**

This is a strong opening with compelling world-building, intriguing mystery, and evocative prose. The pacing is generally well-managed, and the tension builds effectively. The main weakness is the lack of dialogue (as highlighted in the dialogue report) and a slightly slow start.

**Concise Critique (3 points):**

2. **Tighten the Opening:** Streamline the breakfast description to establish the setting more quickly and get to the core mystery sooner.
3. **Expand on Uncertainty:** After the "false positive," linger a beat longer on Leo's internal doubt before dismissing the anomaly.



**Report Summary:**
