Consider condensing these observations or weaving them more organically into Leo’s actions/internal thoughts.

The spikes in Leo’s physiological state (pulse quickening, etc.) are good, but need to be more frequent and impactful.

This happens a couple of times – a potential complication arises, then is swiftly resolved, preventing true tension from building. Allow these minor issues to linger *slightly* longer, creating a sense of escalating pressure.
*   **Paragraph 10 - Strong Peak:** The realization that the signal is a *response* to something Leo did is the strongest moment in the scene – a true tension peak. However, it's somewhat isolated. The preceding build-up could be more focused to amplify this impact.
*   **Ending - Good Pause:** The final line is effective – a chilling realization that leaves the reader wanting more. It provides a satisfying, if unsettling, pause.
*   **Overall:** The scene is *almost* there. The core concept is strong, but the pacing needs tightening and the tension arc needs more deliberate shaping. Reduce the frequency of neutral K-V reports, allow minor complications to linger, and focus the build-up towards the final revelation.
