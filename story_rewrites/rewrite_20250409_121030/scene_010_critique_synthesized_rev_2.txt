## Overall Assessment: Approved with Minor Suggestions

This is a strong piece of writing that effectively establishes a compelling mystery and raises the stakes. The atmosphere is well-crafted, and the characters are distinct. The pacing is generally good, though a few areas could be tightened. The dialogue is functional but could benefit from more nuance and emotional depth. 

**Here are 3 concise points for consideration (though not *required* for approval):**

1. **Dialogue Nuance:** Inject a bit more vulnerability or urgency into Anya's dialogue, particularly when interacting with Leo.
2. **Pacing in Exchange:** Tighten the initial question/answer exchange with Leo after his emergence. Focus on *how* he says things, not just *what* he says.
3. **Seeker's Distress:** Show, don't just tell, the Seeker's distress. Describe its actions or changes in behavior alongside its warnings.



I mark this as **Approved**. I'm eager to see where you take the story next!
