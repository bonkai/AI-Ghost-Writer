## Decision: Approved with Minor Tweaks

This is a strong piece of writing that effectively establishes a compelling premise and builds tension. The world-building is evocative, the characters are well-defined (even the silent ones!), and the stakes are clearly established. 

**Minor Tweaks (to elevate it further):**

1. **Pace the Diagnostic Phase:** Condense the two-day diagnosis. Show Kael's distrust through *action* and brief interactions rather than prolonged observation.
2. **Subtext for Kael & Anya:** Add a moment of non-verbal communication between Kael and Anya as the situation escalates to solidify their dynamic.
3. **Streamline Final Exposition:** End on a more visceral image or Anya's immediate reaction to the signal's effect on someone, rather than fully explaining the threat of "assimilation" in the final paragraphs.



The writing is already very good, and these tweaks would simply polish it to an even higher level. The core story is compelling, and the atmosphere is effectively created.
