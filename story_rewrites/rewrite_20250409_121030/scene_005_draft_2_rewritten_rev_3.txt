## Scene 5: Echoes in the Rust

**Setting:** The skeletal remains of a pre-Collapse factory district. Twisted metal towers against a bruised, overcast sky. Dust devils swirl through the debris.

**KAEL:** (Scanning the sky) This is a waste of time. A good way to get ambushed. Let’s turn back.

Anya runs a gloved hand over a circular scorch mark on the base of a half-collapsed metal tower. The mark radiates outwards, a precise burn unlike any energy discharge she’s seen. Her scanner beeps rapidly, displaying fluctuating energy readings.

**ANYA:** The signal’s strongest here. And it’s resonating with these marks. Multiple occurrences. Focused energy.

**KAEL:** Unstable conduits, maybe? I haven’t seen anything cause a burn like this.

**ANYA:** No. This isn’t random. It’s deliberate. The signal’s coming from up there.

The humming sound, barely audible before, intensifies near the tower. Kael keeps glancing upwards, weapon raised. 

**SEEKER (V.O.):** Negative. Systems… degrading. Energy fluctuations… increasing. I’m detecting… anomalies. Residual energy signatures… unlike anything I’ve encountered. Layered. Echoes.

**ANYA:** Echoes of what?

**SEEKER (V.O.):** Unknown. Centered on the tower. Affecting my sensors. Interfering with readings. And… the echoes are strengthening.

Anya and Kael exchange a look. Anya starts towards the tower, Kael reluctantly following.

**KAEL:** This is a trap. I’m telling you.

**ANYA:** Maybe. But I need to know what’s causing these fluctuations. It's affecting the grid. If it gets worse…

They reach the base of the tower. The scorch marks are more pronounced here, almost covering the surface. Anya scans with her device. The readings spike, then fluctuate wildly.

**ANYA:** The signal’s strongest up there, but the structure is unstable.

Suddenly, Seeker’s voice cuts through the static, laced with urgency.

**SEEKER (V.O.):** Warning! Energy surge detected! Multiple sources! The echoes are intensifying! They’re… forming a pattern! A repeating sequence… images! Fragmented, but… recognizable.

**KAEL:** Images? What images?

Anya’s device displays a chaotic mess of static, then resolves momentarily into a flickering image: a sleek, futuristic product – a handheld device – surrounded by vibrant colors. The image breaks apart, replaced by static.

Anya’s breath hitches. A strange disorientation washes over her. She touches a scorch mark, and a jolt of static electricity surges through her arm, knocking the scanner from her grip. She stumbles back, clutching her head, her vision blurring. For a long moment, she’s lost, struggling to regain her bearings. 

She slowly lowers her hands, her gaze fixed on the spot where the scanner fell. The static on the device flares, resolving again into another image: a smiling family gathered around a holographic display. The image is gone in a blink.

**ANYA:** (Whispering, eyes unfocused) Geometric shapes… colors… Old advertisements. For consumer goods. Things we haven't seen in decades. Distorted, overlaid with static.

Another image flickers, clearer this time: a pristine, futuristic city, gleaming under a bright sky. The image vanishes, replaced by static. Anya’s breath comes in ragged gasps. She feels a growing sense of dread, a disorienting sense of familiarity mixed with profound loss.

**ANYA:** (Whispering, a tremor in her voice) It’s… like looking at something I *should* recognize… a place… a life… but can’t.

She stares at the glitching screen, her face pale. The images flash faster, a chaotic stream of fragmented memories.

**ANYA:** (Whispering, barely audible) What… what is this place? And why does it feel… like a warning?

Kael raises his weapon, scanning the sky. Three Sky-Scavs are visible, descending rapidly.

**KAEL:** Sky-Scavs inbound!

Anya doesn’t react, lost in the fragmented images. The scene ends with Anya staring at the screen, the Sky-Scavs closing in, their screeching cries echoing through the ruins. The fragmented images continue to flicker, a ghost of a past she can’t quite grasp, a growing sense of dread settling over her.
