## Decision: Approved with Minor Tweaks

This is a strong scene with a compelling mystery and good tension building. The dialogue and pacing are generally well-handled.

**Minor Tweaks (1-3 points):**

1. **Tighten the Opening:** Trim the initial dialogue between Kael and Anya to get to the discovery of the scorch marks quicker.
2. **Integrate Threats:** Find a way to connect the Sky-Scavs more directly to the energy surge or the images – either as a consequence of the surge or as an attraction to it.
3. **Subtlety with Anya's Reactions:**  Reduce some of the more direct statements about feeling lost memories. Focus on *showing* her disorientation through physical reactions and fragmented thoughts.



Overall, this scene is ready to move forward. The suggestions above are primarily aimed at polishing an already solid foundation.
