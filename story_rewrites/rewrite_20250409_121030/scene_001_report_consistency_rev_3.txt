## Report: Passed, with Notes

This draft is a strong opening! It effectively establishes Leo's character, the setting, and the initial unsettling feeling that something is wrong. The pacing is good, building tension gradually. The descriptions are vivid and create a clear picture of the sterile, controlled environment Leo inhabits.

**Here's a breakdown of why it works and some minor notes:**

* **Strong Character Introduction:** We immediately understand Leo's personality - a meticulous, control-oriented individual who values efficiency and a frictionless existence. The details about his breakfast and apartment reinforce this.
* **Effective World-Building:** The description of the city and its technology subtly establishes the setting as a highly advanced, possibly utopian (or dystopian) society. The lack of "grit" is a particularly evocative detail.
* **Rising Tension:** The glitches, the fractured memories, and the growing tremor are all well-executed techniques for building suspense. The contrast between the "perfect" diagnostics and Leo's internal experience is key.
* **Good Use of Sensory Detail:** The descriptions of the cinnamon aroma, the cool chrome, and the visual glitches all contribute to a strong immersive experience.
* **Intriguing Mystery:** The draft successfully raises questions about the nature of the glitches and the significance of the fractured memories.

**Minor Notes/Suggestions (mostly stylistic):**

* **"Inefficient" - a bit repetitive:** While emphasizing Leo’s values, the repeated use of “efficient/inefficient” could be varied. Perhaps describe sunlight as "messy" or "unpredictable" instead.
* **Tremor - consistency:** The tremor starts subtle, then is “pronounced.” Maintaining a consistent level of detail about its progression would be helpful. Is it always in his hand? Does it spread?
* **"Ghost in the machine" - cliché:** While not *bad*, this phrase is quite common. Consider a more original metaphor.
* **Pacing – slightly slow in the breakfast section:** The detailed description of breakfast, while establishing character, could be slightly condensed to move the plot forward a bit quicker.



**Overall:** This is a solid start to the story. The writing is strong, the character is compelling, and the mystery is intriguing.  **Passed.** I'm eager to see where you take it.
