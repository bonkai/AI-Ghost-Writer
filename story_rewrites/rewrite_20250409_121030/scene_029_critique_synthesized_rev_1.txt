## Final Decision: Needs More Work

**Concise Critique:**

1. **Dialogue Refinement:** The Seeker’s dialogue is *too* repetitive and flat, hindering pacing and tension. Vary sentence structure and add subtle cues of desperation. Anya’s sharpness also needs to be more present *in* her dialogue.
2. **Pacing – Condense Questioning:** The back-and-forth questioning of the Seeker feels drawn out. Tighten the dialogue to maintain momentum leading to the cliffhanger.
3. **Show, Don't Tell (Character & Emotion):** Replace stage directions explaining emotional states (e.g., "Anya felt strained") with *showing* those emotions through dialogue, action, and body language.



While the core concept and writing are strong, addressing these points will elevate the scene’s impact and create a more compelling reading experience.
