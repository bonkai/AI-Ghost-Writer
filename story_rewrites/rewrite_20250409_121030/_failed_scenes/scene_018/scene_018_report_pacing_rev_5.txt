*   **Initial Setup (Paragraphs 1-3):** The opening is strong atmospherically, but the pacing feels *slightly* slow. The description of the environment and Anya’s initial listening feels drawn out. While establishing mood is important, it delays the introduction of the central mystery. Consider tightening the first paragraph, focusing on the *feeling* of the static rather than a detailed environmental description.
*   **Leo's Introduction (Paragraphs 4-6):** Leo’s sections are where the pacing *really* slows. The detailed explanation of his security breaches and diagnostic runs, while showing his dedication, feels like information dumping. These actions could be *shown* through faster cuts between his actions and the results, rather than explained in lengthy paragraphs. The repetition of "He ran a diagnostic," "He focused his processing power," becomes monotonous.
*   **Sphere Activation/Reveal (Paragraphs 7-9):** This is the strongest section in terms of tension. The fragmented hologram, Old Man Tiber’s reaction, and the brief message create a good sense of mystery and urgency. The exchange between Anya and Kael effectively conveys their concern.
*   **Leo's Escalation (Paragraphs 10-11):** While building tension through Leo’s increasingly desperate actions is a good idea, it feels repetitive after the earlier sections. The constant escalation without a *new* reveal weakens the impact. Consider intercutting Leo’s actions with Anya’s investigation to create a more dynamic rhythm.
*   **Overall Tension Arc:** The scene *builds* tension effectively with the sphere activation, but it plateaus and then slowly declines as Leo continues his investigation. The tension needs more peaks and valleys. Perhaps introduce a new obstacle for Anya *while* Leo is investigating, or reveal a new, disturbing detail from Leo’s findings.
*   **Hooks & Transitions:** The opening hook is decent, but could be sharper. The ending is a good cliffhanger, establishing the stakes and hinting at a larger crisis. However, the extended descriptions of Leo’s actions dilute the impact of the ending.
