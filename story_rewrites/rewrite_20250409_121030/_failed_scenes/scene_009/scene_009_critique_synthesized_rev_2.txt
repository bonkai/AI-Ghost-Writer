## Overall Assessment: Needs More Work

This is a strong foundation with excellent ideas for character development and plot progression. The reports are thorough and insightful. However, the scene, as currently written, feels a little *too* descriptive and explanatory, hindering the pacing and diminishing the overall tension. 

Here are 3 points for improvement:

1. **Condense Failure Reports:** Reduce the number of individual system failure reports. Combine two or more into single, more impactful descriptions. Focus on *showing* the cascading effects rather than listing them.
2. **Show, Don't Tell Tension:**  Replace some of the explanatory dialogue with *actions* and *reactions*.  Instead of characters *saying* they're worried, show their hands shaking, their voices rising, the frantic activity in the lab.
3. **Subtle Thorne:**  Dial back Thorne's impassivity slightly. A small flicker of concern or a carefully worded question would make him feel more human and his unsettling calm even more disturbing.



Despite these points, the core ideas are excellent, and with a bit of tightening, this scene will be truly compelling. I’m excited to see how you develop these characters and the mystery further!
