## Decision: Approved with Minor Revisions

This is a strong scene and largely "Passed" as is. The reveal of Simulife is impactful, the tension is well-managed, and the writing is evocative. 

However, to elevate it further, address these minor points:

1. **Vary Kael's Physicality:** Replace one instance of him tightening his grip with a different reaction – a shift in weight, a focused gaze, a quick scan of the environment.
2. **Show, Don't Tell (Dialogue/Internal Thought):**  Replace Anya’s direct statements (“I don’t know,” “What does it mean?”) and her stating the scientists are worried with internal monologue or evocative descriptions of her reaction.  Let the visuals and data speak for themselves.
3. **Tighten the Post-Reveal:** Condense the final section (Anya’s collapse) slightly to maintain momentum and avoid redundancy. A more gradual descent into realization before the physical reaction will feel more earned.



Implementing these minor changes will polish an already excellent scene and maximize its impact on the reader.
