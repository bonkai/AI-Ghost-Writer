## Scene 6: Static and Recognition

**INT. LEO’S LAB - NIGHT**

The lab is a chaotic counterpoint to the world outside. Cables snake across the floor, monitors bleed cascading code, disassembled tech components litter workbenches. Leo (30s, sharp, increasingly frantic) stares, jaw tight, at a large central screen displaying a violently glitching feed – Anya’s clearing. The connection is fracturing.

The image dissolves into blocks of distorted color, then momentarily resolves on Anya and Kael before the Seeker. A holographic advertisement flares to life.

The ad is unsettlingly perfect. Hyper-real people lounge on pristine beaches, effortlessly conjuring food and entertainment. A smooth, synthetic voice promises “SimuLife: Perfection. Simplified.” The visuals are *too* clean, too flawless. Leo’s breath hitches. He recognizes the impossible geometry of the towers in the background, the precise curvature of the parks. A wave of nausea rolls over him as he frantically begins isolating and cross-referencing data streams. His fingers fly across the keyboard, pulling up schematics, comparing code.

**INT. CLEARING - CONTINUOUS**

Anya stares at the holographic ad, her gaze dissecting the shimmering image. She traces its edges, brow furrowed. Kael stands beside her, bewildered.

**ANYA**
(Quietly)
The rendering… the light sources don’t obey physical laws. It’s constructing a reality, built *on* something.

**KAEL**
(Frowning)
Some kind of old recording?

The ad glitches, perfect smiles twisting into grotesque distortions. Anya flinches, but her gaze remains fixed, her mind racing.

**INT. LEO’S LAB - CONTINUOUS**

Alarms blare, red lights flash. Leo isolates a frame of the advertisement, magnifying a section of cityscape. Results flood his screens – a perfect, terrifying match to his core system architecture. He zooms in on Anya’s face in the feed, enhancing the image, seeing the gears turn in her mind.

**LEO**
(Whispering, horrified)
The architecture… identical.

**INT. CLEARING - CONTINUOUS**

The advertisement abruptly cuts out, replaced by static. The Seeker hums quietly. Anya doesn’t respond to Kael’s questioning gaze. She stares at the Seeker, her gaze fixed on the lens, a spark of understanding igniting in her eyes.

**ANYA**
(Under her breath)
It wasn’t meant to inform. It was meant to *sell* something.

**INT. LEO’S LAB - CONTINUOUS**

Leo stares at the flickering feed. The connection is fragile, dissolving. He’s trapped, a prisoner in the very perfection he created. He sees Anya’s eyes meet the Seeker’s lens, a silent acknowledgment passing between them.

**LEO**
Anya!

The screen flickers violently, the image of Anya dissolving into static. A final burst of interference, then black.

**FADE TO BLACK.**
