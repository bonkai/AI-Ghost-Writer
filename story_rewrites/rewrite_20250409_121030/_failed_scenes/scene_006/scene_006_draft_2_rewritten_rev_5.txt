## Scene 6: Static and Recognition

**INT. LEO’S LAB - NIGHT**

Cables snake across the floor, monitors bleed cascading code, disassembled tech components litter workbenches. Leo (30s, sharp, increasingly frantic) stares, jaw tight, at a large central screen displaying a violently glitching feed – Anya’s clearing. The connection is fracturing, spitting out fragmented images – a flash of Anya, Kael, the Seeker – before dissolving into a holographic advertisement.

The ad begins, not with perfection, but with a jarring dissonance – pristine beaches fractured by impossible angles, conjured food shimmering with an unnatural sheen. A smooth, synthetic voice promises “SimuLife: Perfection. Simplified.” Leo’s breath hitches. He recognizes the impossible geometry, fingers flying across the keyboard, frantically isolating and cross-referencing data streams. A wave of nausea rolls over him as results flood his screens. He clenches a fist, knuckles white.

**INT. CLEARING - CONTINUOUS**

Anya stares at the holographic ad, her gaze dissecting the shimmering image. She traces its edges, brow furrowed, ignoring Kael’s questioning look. 

**KAEL**
(Frowning)
Some kind of old recording?

The ad glitches, perfect smiles twisting into grotesque distortions. Anya flinches, a sharp intake of breath, but her gaze remains fixed, her mind racing. She squints, tilting her head, a flicker of confusion crossing her face. A muscle jumps in her jaw.

**INT. LEO’S LAB - CONTINUOUS**

Alarms blare, red lights flash. Leo isolates a frame of the advertisement, magnifying a section of cityscape. The results are terrifying. A perfect, one-to-one match with his core system architecture. He zooms in on Anya’s face in the feed, enhancing the image, seeing the realization begin to dawn in her eyes.

**INT. CLEARING - CONTINUOUS**

The advertisement abruptly cuts out, replaced by static. The Seeker hums quietly. Anya doesn’t respond to Kael’s questioning gaze. She stares at the Seeker, her gaze fixed on the lens. A spark of understanding ignites in her eyes. She breathes, a small, sharp intake of air.

*It wasn’t meant to…to show us something…* she thinks, fragmented, *to sell…to sell us…*

**INT. LEO’S LAB - CONTINUOUS**

Leo stares at the flickering feed. The connection is dissolving, fracturing. He’s trapped, a prisoner within the very perfection he created. He sees Anya’s eyes meet the Seeker’s lens, a silent acknowledgment passing between them. 

**LEO**
Anya…no…this isn’t… everything. Everything we built…

The screen flickers violently, the image of Anya dissolving into static. A ripple of distortion spreads across her face, then across the entire feed. A final burst of interference, then black. The distortion expands, consuming the lab in a wash of digital decay.

**FADE TO BLACK.**
