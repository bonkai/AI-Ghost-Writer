*   **Tiber’s outburst is strong, but a bit late.** His warning feels like a sudden exposition dump. Foreshadowing his suspicions earlier – a muttered comment, a worried glance at the Seeker – would make it more impactful.

*   **Anya’s realization (“It wasn’t damaged. It was a beacon…”) feels rushed.** Give her a beat to process the information and connect the dots. A moment of internal struggle would add depth to her character.
