## Overall Assessment: Needs More Work

**Concise Critique:**

1. **Pacing:** The beginning is *too* slow and descriptive. Condense the breakfast scene and establish conflict earlier.
2. **Dialogue:** The complete absence of dialogue feels unnatural. Even a brief interaction would add dynamism and reveal character.
3. **Climax:** The climax (the glitch in the Archive) feels rushed after the slow build-up. Expand on the visual and sensory experience of the glitch to create a more impactful and unsettling moment.



While the writing is strong and the core concept is intriguing, addressing these points will significantly enhance the story’s momentum, character development, and overall impact.
