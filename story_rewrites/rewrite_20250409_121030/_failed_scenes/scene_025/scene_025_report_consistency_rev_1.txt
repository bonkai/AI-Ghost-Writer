**Minor Notes (for consideration, not necessarily corrections):**

**Overall:**

This is a strong draft that effectively sets the stage for the next phase of the story. The writing is engaging, the atmosphere is well-crafted, and the mystery is compelling. The minor notes are simply suggestions for refinement and don't detract from the overall quality of the work.
