*   **Initial Atmosphere (Paragraphs 1-2):** The opening is strong in establishing mood, but the detailed description of the forest and the Seeker’s deterioration feels slightly *too* prolonged. While atmospheric, it slows the momentum before any real conflict emerges. Consider condensing some of the descriptive elements.
*   **Rising Tension (Paragraphs 3-7):** The tension builds effectively with the Seeker’s increasingly fragmented speech and physical decline. The short, declarative sentences (“Structural integrity compromised,” “Boundaries thinning.”) contribute to a sense of urgency. However, the repetition of “Remnants…” and the extended walk *without* a clear threat being revealed risks diluting the tension.
*   **Peak & Reveal (Paragraphs 8-10):** The sudden stop, the beetle, and the reveal of the distortion are well-executed. This is the strongest part of the scene, creating a genuine moment of suspense.
*   **Collapse & Cliffhanger (Paragraphs 11-12):** The Seeker’s collapse is impactful. The final lines – particularly the pulsing core – are a good cliffhanger, but feel a bit tacked on. Integrating the core’s failing state *during* the Seeker’s final moments might amplify the dread.
*   **Overall:** The scene has a good tension arc, but it’s uneven. The initial and middle sections could benefit from tighter pacing. Consider streamlining descriptions and focusing on *showing* the Seeker's deterioration through its actions rather than *telling* us about it. The ending is strong, but could be more seamlessly integrated.
