*   **Kai's Dialogue is Functional, Not Revealing:** Kai's lines ("Another fluctuation…", "It’s always growing…") are largely exposition delivery. While accurate to his stressed state, they don’t reveal much about *him* as a character. Consider adding a line that reveals his frustration, desperation, or even a hint of hopelessness beyond simply stating the facts. Perhaps a muttered curse under his breath, or a brief, defeated admission of how close they are to failure.
*   **Anya's Internal State Needs More Vocalization:** Anya does a lot of *thinking* and *feeling* internally. While effective for building suspense, it leads to her spoken dialogue being somewhat flat. She asks questions and states observations, but rarely *reacts* with emotional language. Even a small line acknowledging her fear or anxiety would make her feel more present.
*   **“What was it doing out in the open?” feels a bit redundant.** The previous lines establish the Seeker hasn’t left the Dead Cities in months. The question feels like telling the reader something they already know. Consider rephrasing to focus on *why* the Seeker is doing something unusual.
*   **The Voice – Opportunity for Subtext:** The voice entering Anya’s mind is a strong moment, but the lines ("Anomaly detected," "Initiating contact") are very direct. Consider if some of the meaning could be conveyed through *how* the voice sounds – its tone, cadence, or even the *feeling* it evokes in Anya. Is it curious? Threatening? Ancient and indifferent? Let Anya's reaction *show* us more than simply stating the lines.
*   **Repetitive Emphasis on the Seeker:** The constant reiteration of the Seeker watching Anya ("It hadn’t acknowledged her…", "The Seeker, bathed in…") feels a little heavy-handed. Trust the reader to remember the initial establishment of the Seeker’s gaze.
*   **Anya's stumble and inability to answer is a good physical reaction, but could be strengthened with a brief internal thought.** A quick glimpse into her mind as she struggles to process what she's heard would add impact.
