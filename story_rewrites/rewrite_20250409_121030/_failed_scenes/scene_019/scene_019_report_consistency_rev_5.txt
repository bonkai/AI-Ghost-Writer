* **The "Memory" Reveal:** This is the strongest element. Introducing a memory *outside* the simulation is a fantastic twist and opens up exciting possibilities.

**Areas for Potential Improvement (Minor):**

* **Kael's Role:** Kael feels slightly passive in this scene. While his concern is established, he doesn't actively contribute much to the plot. Consider giving him a small action or observation that hints at his awareness of something being amiss.  Even a subtle shift in his behavior could strengthen his role.

Perhaps a brief internal monologue explaining *why* he's overriding the warnings would add depth. (e.g., "The warnings screamed of protocol violations, but this wasn't a threat he could quantify. He bypassed them, needing to see what lay beneath.")

While poetic, consider grounding it with a more concrete detail. (e.g., "a cascade of corrupted code briefly illuminated the data stream, a flicker of *otherness* pushing through the simulation’s boundaries.")



**Overall:**

This is a very strong draft. The writing is evocative, the pacing is excellent, and the mystery is compelling. The introduction of the memory element is a brilliant move that elevates the story.  I recommend making the minor adjustments suggested above to further polish the draft, but overall, it's a clear "Passed."
