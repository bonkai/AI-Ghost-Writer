*   **K-V's Dialogue – Robotic & Repetitive:** While K-V is an AI, the constant "Unit requesting…" and immediate responses feel overly formal and lack nuance. It's functional, but doesn't reveal much about *this* particular AI’s personality (if any). Consider varying sentence structure or adding brief processing indicators beyond the initial pause. (“Analyzing… results pending.” or “Request acknowledged. Processing…”)
*   **Leo's Murmurs – Internal vs. External:** Leo frequently murmurs to himself ("Focus on signal harmonics," "Increase signal amplification"). While this can show focus, it’s happening *a lot*. Is it necessary for the reader to know *every* thought process? Some of these could be implied through action or removed entirely.
*   **Information Exchange – Too Direct:** The dialogue is very transactional. K-V asks for data, Leo gives commands. There’s little subtext or emotional layering. Even a subtle hint of Leo’s desperation or K-V’s analytical detachment would add depth.
*   **Ignoring Alarms – Repetitive & Lacking Stakes:** The repeated ignoring of perimeter breaches is becoming a pattern. While it establishes Leo's obsession, it’s losing impact. Consider *showing* the consequences of his neglect – a brief system failure, a stressed generator – to raise the stakes.
*   **K-V's Requests – Building Suspicion:** K-V's requests for organic compounds are important plot points, but they're stated plainly. A slightly more indirect approach – perhaps framing it as a standard diagnostic query, then subtly emphasizing the focus on organic matter – could create more suspense.
*   **Leo's Recognition – Could be More Dramatic:** The moment Leo recognizes the repeating sequence feels a bit flat. A brief internal monologue, a physical reaction (a gasp, a tremor), or a more evocative description of the pattern itself would heighten the impact.
