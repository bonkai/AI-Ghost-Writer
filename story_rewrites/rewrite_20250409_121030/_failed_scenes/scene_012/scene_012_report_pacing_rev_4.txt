The introduction of the perimeter breach is *effective* – the consistent ignoring of it creates a low-level, persistent anxiety. However, the repeated acknowledgment with a keystroke feels a little repetitive. Perhaps vary the response – a muttered curse, a dismissive gesture relayed to K-V.
*   **Peak Tension (Paragraphs 8-10):** The request for organic compounds is a strong beat, and the realization that the signal is a *response* is a good peak. The quickening pulse and lack of blinking heighten the immediacy.
