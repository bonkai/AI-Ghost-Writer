*   **Distrust of Old Tech - Underdeveloped:** The scene *hints* at distrust of the past/old tech through Kael’s dismissal of Anya’s grandmother’s stories, but it doesn’t deeply *ingrain* it as the plan requires. We need more explicit connection between the past and present dangers – perhaps a cautionary tale *directly* linked to failed old tech, or a visible consequence of relying on something salvaged.
*   **Resource Scarcity - Show, Don't Tell:** The scene states they are low on resin, but doesn’t *show* the impact of scarcity. Consider visual details: are patches visibly failing elsewhere? Are workers rationing materials? A stronger sense of desperation will heighten the stakes.
*   **Anya's Internal Conflict - Needs Amplification:** Anya's longing is present, but feels passive. The flicker of the listening post is a good start, but needs to be connected more strongly to her internal state and her questioning of the present. Is she actively *seeking* something beyond the haze? Does she believe the stories? This is the core of her character and needs more development.
