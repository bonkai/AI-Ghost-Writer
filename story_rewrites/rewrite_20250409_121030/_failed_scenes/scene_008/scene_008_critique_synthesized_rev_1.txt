## Overall Assessment: Needs More Work

While the core story and concepts are strong, the pacing and tension need refinement. Here's a concise critique:

1. **Show, Don't Tell:** Reduce reliance on K-V *stating* actions and instead *show* discoveries through Anya's reactions and the revealed data.
2. **Vary Pacing:** Break up lengthy analytical sequences with moments of Anya’s internal thought, action, or emotional response to allow key reveals to breathe.
3. **Amplify Tension:** Inject more immediate stakes and emotional weight into the scenes by focusing on Anya's subjective experience and allowing key moments to linger before launching into technical explanations.



The writing is solid, but these adjustments will elevate the story from informative to truly gripping.
