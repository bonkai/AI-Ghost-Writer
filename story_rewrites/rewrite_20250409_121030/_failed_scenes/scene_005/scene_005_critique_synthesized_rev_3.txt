## Overall Assessment: Approved with Minor Suggestions

This is a strong scene with compelling mystery and atmosphere. The pacing is generally good, and the tension builds effectively. The core issue lies in the repetitive delivery of the image revelations and a slightly weak cliffhanger. 

**Concise Critique (3 points):**

1. **Vary Image Delivery:** Break up the "flicker-image-reaction" pattern to maintain momentum and impact.
2. **Interweave External Threat:** Integrate the Sky-Scavs’ approach earlier to heighten pressure.
3. **Strengthen Cliffhanger:** Give Anya a brief reaction to the Sky-Scavs to create a more compelling and impactful ending.
