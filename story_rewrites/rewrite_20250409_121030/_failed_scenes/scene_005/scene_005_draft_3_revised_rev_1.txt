## Scene 5: Echoes in the Rust

**Setting:** The skeletal remains of a pre-Collapse factory district. Twisted metal towers against a bruised, overcast sky. Dust devils swirl through the debris.

**KAEL:** (Scanning the sky) This is a waste of time. A good way to get ambushed. I still say we turn back.

**ANYA:** (Grimly) Not yet.

Anya runs a gloved hand over a circular scorch mark on the base of a half-collapsed metal tower. The mark radiates outwards, a precise burn unlike any energy discharge she’s seen. Her scanner beeps rapidly, displaying fluctuating energy readings.

**ANYA:** The signal’s strongest here. And it’s resonating with these marks. Multiple occurrences. Focused energy. Several times.

**KAEL:** Unstable conduits, maybe? Though I haven’t seen anything cause *this* kind of burn.

**ANYA:** No. This isn’t random. It’s deliberate. (She indicates the tower) The signal’s coming from up there.

The humming sound, barely audible before, intensifies near the tower. Kael keeps glancing upwards, weapon raised.

**KAEL:** That’s Sky-Scav territory. They’ll be on us fast.

**ANYA:** (Activating her comms) Seeker, report. Any change in your status?

Static crackles, then Seeker’s voice, strained and glitching.

**SEEKER (V.O.):** Negative. Systems… degrading. Energy fluctuations… increasing. I’m detecting… anomalies. Residual energy signatures… unlike anything I’ve encountered. Layered. Echoes.

**ANYA:** Echoes of what?

**SEEKER (V.O.):** Unknown. Centered on the tower. Affecting my sensors. Interfering with readings. And… the echoes are strengthening.

Anya and Kael exchange a look. Anya starts towards the tower, Kael reluctantly following.

**KAEL:** This is a trap. I’m telling you.

**ANYA:** Maybe. But I need to know what’s causing these fluctuations. It's affecting the grid. If it gets worse…

They reach the base of the tower. The scorch marks are more pronounced here, almost covering the surface. Anya scans with her device. The readings spike, then fluctuate wildly.

**ANYA:** The signal’s strongest up there, but the structure is unstable.

Suddenly, Seeker’s voice cuts through the static, laced with urgency.

**SEEKER (V.O.):** Warning! Energy surge detected! Multiple sources! The echoes are intensifying! They’re… forming a pattern! A repeating sequence… images! Fragmented, but… recognizable.

**KAEL:** Images? What images?

Anya’s device displays a chaotic mess of static, then resolves momentarily into a flickering image: a sleek, futuristic product – a handheld device – surrounded by vibrant colors. The image breaks apart, replaced by static, then reforms, showing a smiling family gathered around a holographic display. The image is gone in a blink.

**ANYA:** (Whispering, eyes fixed on the device) Geometric shapes… colors… It’s like… advertisements. Old advertisements. For consumer goods. Things we haven't seen in decades. But… distorted. Overlaid with static.

Kael raises his weapon, scanning the sky.

**KAEL:** Sky-Scavs! Three of them, inbound fast!

Anya ignores him, transfixed. A strange disorientation washes over her. The images seem… vaguely familiar, like half-remembered dreams. She touches a scorch mark, and a jolt of static electricity runs through her arm.

**ANYA:** (Confused, almost to herself) It’s… like looking at something I *should* remember… but can’t. A life I never lived.

The images on her device flash faster, coalescing momentarily into a clear picture of a pristine, futuristic city. The image is gone in an instant, replaced by static.

**ANYA:** (Whispering) What… what is this place? What have we forgotten?

She stumbles back, clutching her head. The fragmented images continue to flicker, hinting at a past that is both alien and disturbingly familiar.

**ANYA:** (A growing sense of dread in her voice) And why does it feel like… a warning?

The scene ends with Anya staring at the glitching screen, the Sky-Scavs closing in, their screeching cries echoing through the ruins. The fragmented images continue to flicker, a haunting reminder of a forgotten past.
