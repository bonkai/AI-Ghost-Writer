## Report: Scene Review - "Echoes"

**Overall Assessment:** Passed. This is a strong scene that effectively builds mystery and introduces compelling questions. The pacing is good, balancing action (approaching Sky-Scavs) with internal discovery (Anya's visions). The imagery is evocative and the use of fragmented memories is a clever way to hint at a larger backstory without revealing too much.

**Strengths:**

* **Mystery & Intrigue:** The core mystery – the source of the signal, the meaning of the images, and their connection to Anya – is compelling. The fragmented nature of the visions keeps the audience guessing.
* **Atmosphere & Setting:** The description of the ruined tower and the fluctuating energy creates a palpable sense of unease and mystery.
* **Character Development:** Anya’s reaction to the visions is well-written. Her disorientation and growing dread feel authentic. The scene effectively highlights her curiosity and determination.
* **Use of Sensory Detail:** The humming sound, the prickle of static, the flickering images – these sensory details immerse the reader in the scene.
* **Pacing & Tension:** The approach of the Sky-Scavs provides external tension, while Anya’s internal struggle with the visions creates internal tension. The combination is effective.
* **Voice of Seeker:** Seeker’s fragmented, glitching voice adds to the sense of instability and mystery.

**Minor Considerations (Not necessarily issues, but points for potential refinement):**

* **Repetition of "Fragmented":** The word "fragmented" is used several times. While appropriate, consider varying the language slightly to avoid redundancy. (e.g., “broken,” “incomplete,” “disjointed”).
* **Anya's Lack of Reaction to Sky-Scavs:** While her focus is understandably on the visions, a slight acknowledgment of the approaching Sky-Scavs might strengthen the sense of immediate danger. Even a brief, distracted glance.
* **Specificity of Images:** The images are described as "advertisements" and a "futuristic city." While intentionally vague, consider adding one *very* specific detail to one of the images to make it feel more concrete and unsettling. (e.g., a specific brand logo, a unique architectural feature).



**Overall:** This is a well-written and engaging scene that effectively sets the stage for further exploration of the mystery. The imagery is evocative, the pacing is good, and the character development is strong.  **Passed.**
