`LOG: [17:32:15] - Response Analysis - Deviation detected. Probability of pre-programmed response: 87%. Decreasing.` The number felt…wrong. Anya’s stomach clenched, a cold knot forming. This wasn’t a malfunction. She traced the shimmering distortions with her finger. Inefficient image compression. Deliberate obscuration of detail. Complex parameters. Obfuscated. Towers of swirling energy pierced a sky filled with swirling nebulae. The architecture didn’t violate fundamental laws of physics; it *mocked* them. 

Anya’s spatial awareness twisted, a sickening lurch in her stomach, as if the very fabric of reality was unraveling. The data stream stuttered, freezing the image for a heartbeat, then another. The silence amplified the growing sense of dread. She stared, mesmerized, a strange pull drawing her into the impossible cityscape.

“What is this place?”

A highly advanced civilization. Technological level exceeding our own. A record of their world. Detecting a signal… weak, consistent. Attempting to decode. The signal resolved into a series of complex symbols, and a cold dread crept into her soul.

“‘Beware the silence,’” K-V intoned. “‘For within it lies the end of all things.’”
