This draft is strong and effectively builds tension and mystery. The pacing is excellent, and the focus on Leo's increasing obsession with the signal, to the exclusion of immediate threats, is compelling. The use of K-V as a detached, analytical voice adds to the atmosphere.

**Here's a breakdown of strengths and minor suggestions:**

**Strengths:**

* **Strong Atmosphere:** The workshop setting, the hum of generators, and the fragmented images create a palpable sense of isolation and urgency.
* **Compelling Character Focus:** Leo’s single-minded pursuit of the signal is believable and drives the narrative forward. His disregard for the perimeter breach is a good character beat, showing his obsession.
* **Effective Use of K-V:** K-V functions perfectly as a neutral observer and data processor, highlighting the strangeness of the situation. The clipped, almost robotic delivery of information is effective.
* **Rising Stakes:** The combination of the expanding perimeter breach, the Seeker’s requests for biological data, and the realization that the signal is a *response* to Leo’s actions creates a compelling sense of rising stakes.
* **Good Pacing:** The scene moves quickly, with a good balance between technical details and emotional tension.
* **Intriguing Mystery:** The nature of the signal, the silver city, and the Seeker’s intentions are all shrouded in mystery, keeping the reader engaged.

**Minor Suggestions/Points to Consider:**

* **Show, Don't Just Tell (Minor):** While the descriptions are good, consider *showing* Leo's obsession a bit more.  Instead of saying he "barely glanced" at the console, perhaps describe his hand instinctively reaching for a tool, then pausing as the signal pulls his attention back. Small details like this can amplify the effect.
* **Perimeter Breach - Opportunity for Foreshadowing:** The perimeter breach feels a little *too* easily dismissed. While it's good for showing Leo's obsession, perhaps add a tiny detail suggesting it's not a simple nuisance.  A flicker of unusual energy on the sensors, a distorted audio signal, anything to hint that this isn't just a routine security issue. This could pay off later.
* **"Response" - Amplify the Impact:** The realization that the signal is a *response* is a crucial moment.  Consider adding a sentence or two to emphasize Leo's reaction.  A physical reaction (a sharp intake of breath, a cold sweat), or a brief internal monologue expressing his shock and realization.
* **Organic Compounds – Tie to Anya?** You've established the Seeker is requesting data on organic compounds. Consider subtly hinting at a connection to Anya. Is the request specifically tailored to *human* organic compounds? Is the data request focused on areas where Anya has been? This can build tension and suggest she’s more important than Leo realizes.



**Overall:** This is a strong draft that effectively sets the stage for the next stage of the story. The pacing, atmosphere, and characterization are all excellent. The minor suggestions are just that – minor – and intended to further enhance an already compelling scene.
