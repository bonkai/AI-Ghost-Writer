## Scene 6: Static and Recognition

**INT. LEO’S LAB - NIGHT**

Cables snake across the floor, monitors bleed cascading code, disassembled tech components litter workbenches. Leo (30s, sharp, increasingly frantic) stares, jaw tight, at a large central screen displaying a violently glitching feed – Anya’s clearing. The connection is fracturing, spitting out fragmented images.

The feed momentarily resolves on Anya and Kael before the Seeker, then explodes into a holographic advertisement. 

The ad is unsettlingly perfect – pristine beaches, effortless conjuring of food and entertainment. A smooth, synthetic voice promises “SimuLife: Perfection. Simplified.” Leo’s breath hitches. He recognizes the impossible geometry of the towers in the background. He frantically begins isolating and cross-referencing data streams, a wave of nausea rolling over him as results flood his screens.

**INT. CLEARING - CONTINUOUS**

Anya stares at the holographic ad, her gaze dissecting the shimmering image. She traces its edges, brow furrowed, ignoring Kael’s questioning look. 

**KAEL**
(Frowning)
Some kind of old recording?

The ad glitches, perfect smiles twisting into grotesque distortions. Anya flinches, but her gaze remains fixed, her mind racing. She squints, tilting her head.

**INT. LEO’S LAB - CONTINUOUS**

Alarms blare, red lights flash. Leo isolates a frame of the advertisement, magnifying a section of cityscape. The results are terrifying. A perfect, one-to-one match with his core system architecture. He zooms in on Anya’s face in the feed, enhancing the image, seeing the realization dawn in her eyes.

**INT. CLEARING - CONTINUOUS**

The advertisement abruptly cuts out, replaced by static. The Seeker hums quietly. Anya doesn’t respond to Kael’s questioning gaze. She stares at the Seeker, her gaze fixed on the lens. A spark of understanding ignites in her eyes. She breathes, a small, sharp intake of air.

**ANYA**
(Under her breath, barely audible)
It wasn’t meant to *show* us something. It was meant to *sell* us something…

**INT. LEO’S LAB - CONTINUOUS**

Leo stares at the flickering feed. The connection is dissolving, fracturing. He’s trapped, a prisoner within the very perfection he created. He sees Anya’s eyes meet the Seeker’s lens, a silent acknowledgment passing between them. 

**LEO**
Anya…no…

The screen flickers violently, the image of Anya dissolving into static. A final burst of interference, then black. A ripple of distortion spreads across the remaining screen, consuming the lab in a wash of digital decay.

**FADE TO BLACK.**
