*   **Initial Atmosphere (Paragraphs 1-2):** Excellent start. The slow, descriptive pacing effectively establishes a sense of unease and foreshadows the impending breakdown. The focus on sensory details (chill, sound of leaves, metallic clicks) is well-executed.
*   **Seeker’s Deterioration (Paragraphs 3-7):** The pacing here is *almost* too consistent. While the gradual breakdown is logical, it risks becoming monotonous. Consider interspersing brief moments of heightened action (a larger piece falling off, a more violent jerk) to break up the descriptive flow and amplify the tension. The repetition of “parameters,” “structural integrity,” etc., while illustrating the Seeker’s failing state, could be streamlined.
*   **Rising Tension (Paragraphs 8-10):** The high-pitched whine is a strong tension-builder, but its introduction feels slightly delayed. Perhaps introduce a *hint* of it earlier, growing in intensity as the Seeker deteriorates. The description of the distortion is effective, but the final sentence ("But I have a feeling we're about to find out.") feels a little on-the-nose. Trust the imagery to speak for itself.
*   **Climax & Pause (Paragraphs 11-12):** The Seeker’s collapse is well-timed and provides a satisfying (if unsettling) climax to this section. The final paragraph, with the static and pulse, is a strong hook, leaving the reader wanting to know what lies beyond the distortion.
*   **Overall:** The scene is generally well-paced, but could benefit from more dynamic shifts in rhythm to heighten the tension during the Seeker’s deterioration. The ending is strong, but a slightly more subtle build-up to the climax could be even more effective.
