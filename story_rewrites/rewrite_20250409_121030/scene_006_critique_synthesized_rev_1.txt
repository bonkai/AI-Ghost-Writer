2. **Tighten Pacing in Middle Section:** Shorten Anya’s analytical dialogue and intersperse it more actively with Leo’s actions to maintain a higher level of tension throughout.
3. **Subtlety & Nuance:** Refine Anya’s "we're the product" line and the “selling a reality” concept to be less on-the-nose. Focus on the *feeling* evoked by the ad and allow the audience to draw their own conclusions.
