*   **Internal Monologue Integration:** The internal monologue, while present, feels somewhat detached. It's presented in blocks of *italicized* text, creating a distance rather than seamlessly weaving the Seeker’s thoughts into the narrative. Integrate these thoughts more organically into descriptive passages and observations.
*   **Show, Don't Tell (Resonance):** The concept of Anya "resonating" with the system is *told* repeatedly, but not *shown*. We need concrete details of *how* her actions stabilize things, beyond just stating it happens. Focus on sensory details – a flicker in the Seeker’s vision, a drop in error messages, a change in the ambient static – to demonstrate this resonance.
*   **Prioritize the Malfunction:** The scene lists multiple malfunctions (atmospheric processors, power reserves) but doesn't fully leverage the escalating crisis for dramatic tension. Focus on 1-2 critical failures and *show* their impact on the Seeker’s functionality – degraded sensor readings, processing delays, loss of data – to emphasize the urgency and connect it directly to Anya's influence.
