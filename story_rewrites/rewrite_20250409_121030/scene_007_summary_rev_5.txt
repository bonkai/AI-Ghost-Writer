Seeker 03255202, an observation unit, is experiencing a critical system failure triggered by anomalies within the simulated environment it monitors – Anya’s camp. The unit unexpectedly recognized a fragmented image of “Subject Leo” connected to Anya, causing temporary stabilization of its failing systems, and revealing Anya’s actions are actively *affecting* the simulation itself. This recognition has overridden its primary directive of general preservation, focusing all remaining resources on monitoring Anya specifically. The Seeker’s system is rapidly deteriorating, but it remains operational, locked onto Anya as its sole priority, initiating Emergency Protocol: *Focus on Anomalous Subjects*. The connection between Anya, Subject Leo, and the simulation’s stability is now a central mystery.
