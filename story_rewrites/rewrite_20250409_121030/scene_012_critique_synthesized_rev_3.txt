## Overall Assessment: Approved with Recommendations

This is a strong scene with a compelling premise and effective atmosphere. The writing is solid, and the core mystery is intriguing. The pacing and tension, while *almost* there, are the areas needing the most refinement. 

**Recommendations (focus for revision):**

1. **Reduce & Integrate Physiological Reporting:** Drastically cut back on K-V's constant reporting of Leo's physiological state. *Show* his obsession through his actions and physical reactions instead.
2. **Prolong Minor Complications:** Allow minor issues (like the perimeter breach) to linger for a beat longer before resolving them. This creates a sense of escalating pressure.
3. **Tighten Pacing & Focus Tension:** Condense the slower sections, particularly in the opening, and focus the build-up towards the final revelation to maximize its impact.



Despite these recommendations, the scene is well-written and engaging, and I approve it with the expectation that these points will be addressed in a subsequent revision.
