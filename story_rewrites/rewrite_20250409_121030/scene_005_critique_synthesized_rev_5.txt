## Overall Assessment: Approved with Minor Adjustments Recommended

This is a strong scene with a compelling mystery and good tension building. The imagery is evocative, and Anya’s internal struggle is well-portrayed. The dialogue and pacing reports highlight areas for refinement, but these are relatively minor and wouldn't fundamentally change the scene's effectiveness. 

**Here's a consolidated summary of recommendations, prioritizing the most impactful:**

1. **Pacing – Tighten the Opening:** Condense the initial descriptive paragraphs and Kael's early warnings to establish the threat more quickly.
2. **Interweave Internal & External Tension:**  More seamlessly blend Anya's internal experience with the external threat of the Sky-Scavs. Let her visions *affect* her reaction to the immediate danger, and vice-versa.
3. **Dialogue – Subtlety & Connection:**  Reduce the expository dialogue (especially Seeker's VO) and focus on showing, not telling. Add more nuance to Kael’s dialogue – show his concern *for* Anya, not just his concern *about* the situation.



**Decision: Approved with Minor Adjustments Recommended.**  Addressing the points above will elevate an already strong scene to an even higher level.
