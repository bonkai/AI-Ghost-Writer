## Scene 6: Static and Recognition

**INT. LEO’S LAB - NIGHT**

Cables snake across the floor, monitors bleed cascading code, disassembled tech components litter workbenches. Leo (30s, sharp, increasingly frantic) stares, jaw tight, at a large central screen displaying a violently glitching feed – Anya’s clearing. The connection is fracturing.

The image dissolves into blocks of distorted color, then momentarily resolves on Anya and Kael before the Seeker. A holographic advertisement flares to life.

The ad is unsettlingly perfect. Hyper-real people lounge on pristine beaches, effortlessly conjuring food and entertainment. A smooth, synthetic voice promises “SimuLife: Perfection. Simplified.” The visuals are *too* clean, too flawless. Leo’s breath hitches. He recognizes the impossible geometry of the towers in the background, the precise curvature of the parks. A wave of nausea rolls over him as he frantically begins isolating and cross-referencing data streams. His fingers fly across the keyboard, pulling up schematics, comparing code. He doesn’t speak, just…stares, then rapidly types.

**INT. CLEARING - CONTINUOUS**

Anya stares at the holographic ad, her gaze dissecting the shimmering image. She traces its edges, brow furrowed. Kael stands beside her, bewildered. She doesn’t respond to his questioning look.

**ANYA**
(Quietly, almost to herself)
The rendering… the light sources don’t obey physical laws. It’s…constructed *on* something.

**KAEL**
(Frowning)
Some kind of old recording?

The ad glitches, perfect smiles twisting into grotesque distortions. Anya flinches, but her gaze remains fixed, her mind racing. She barely registers Kael’s voice.

**INT. LEO’S LAB - CONTINUOUS**

Alarms blare, red lights flash. Leo isolates a frame of the advertisement, magnifying a section of cityscape. Results flood his screens – a perfect, terrifying match to his core system architecture. He zooms in on Anya’s face in the feed, enhancing the image, seeing the gears turn in her mind. He doesn't speak, just stares, his face pale.

**INT. CLEARING - CONTINUOUS**

The advertisement abruptly cuts out, replaced by static. The Seeker hums quietly. Anya doesn’t respond to Kael’s questioning gaze. She stares at the Seeker, her gaze fixed on the lens, a spark of understanding igniting in her eyes. She breathes, a small, sharp intake of air.

**ANYA**
(Under her breath, barely audible)
It wasn’t meant to inform. It was meant to *sell* something…

**INT. LEO’S LAB - CONTINUOUS**

Leo stares at the flickering feed. The connection is fragile, dissolving. He’s trapped, a prisoner in the very perfection he created. He sees Anya’s eyes meet the Seeker’s lens, a silent acknowledgment passing between them. His hand trembles as he reaches for the controls.

**LEO**
Anya…

The screen flickers violently, the image of Anya dissolving into static. A final burst of interference, then black.

**FADE TO BLACK.**
