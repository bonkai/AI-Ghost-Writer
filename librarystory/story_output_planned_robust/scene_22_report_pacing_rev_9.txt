However, Kaelen's initial responses are *too* fragmented too quickly. While strained is good, the immediate fragmentation feels less like effort and more like a narrative shortcut. However, Rhys’s murmured lines feel slightly *too* expository. Show his understanding through action or a more subtle reaction rather than telling us he understands.
*   **Overall Tension Arc:** The scene builds tension effectively, peaking during the vision sequence. However, the tension dips slightly after the initial information reveal. Interspersing reactions and internal thoughts throughout would help maintain a more consistent level of suspense. The scene relies heavily on *telling* us the information is weighty, rather than *showing* its impact through character behavior.
