*   **Initial Momentum:** The opening is strong, establishing urgency with Seris's physical reaction and Rhys's restless energy. Good start to build tension.
*   **Information Dump Risk:** The extended passage from Kaelen (and subsequent dialogue unpacking it) slows the pace considerably. While the information is crucial, delivering it through extended exposition feels static. Consider breaking it up with more immediate reactions or actions.

*   **Delayed Reveal:** The final realization about the Veil is effective, but the pacing to get there feels slightly elongated. The repeated phrasing ("keeping things out…keeping something in") is a little redundant and slows the momentum.
*   **Ending Strength:** The final exchange between Rhys and Seris is solid, establishing a clear direction and raising the stakes. However, the repetition of physical ticks (muscle jumping in jaw) feels slightly overdone.

Overall, the scene is well-constructed, but the pacing could be tightened by interweaving action/reaction more seamlessly with the information delivery. The tension is present, but it doesn't consistently escalate and release in a compelling rhythm.
