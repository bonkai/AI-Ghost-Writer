*   **Ending – Micro-Tension & Hook:** The final paragraph is strong, delivering a satisfying reveal and establishing a clear sense of danger. The fact Seris doesn’t signal her departure is a nice touch, reinforcing her understanding of the situation. However, the scene could benefit from a *slightly* more immediate hook to propel the reader into the next scene. Perhaps a lingering image of Valerius, or a final, unsettling action.
