However, the pauses between Kaelen's "thoughts" sometimes flatten the arc. Consider shortening some of the silent stretches to maintain pressure.
*   **Ending Resonance:** The ending is strong. The shared perception of the petal is a beautiful touch and provides a satisfying, if quiet, resolution of micro-tension. It successfully creates anticipation for the next stage of the journey.
