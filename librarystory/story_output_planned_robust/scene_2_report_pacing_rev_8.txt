*   However, the repeated questioning ("What is this place?", "Who are you?") without substantial answers starts to feel cyclical around paragraph 9. While establishing his desperation, it diminishes the tension. Perhaps intersperse these questions with more active attempts to escape or understand.
*   The final three paragraphs (11-13) deliver a satisfying, if unsettling, climax. The imagery of the hand and the final word, "Forgotten," are particularly effective.
*   Overall, the pacing is generally good, but the middle section (paragraphs 6-9) could benefit from more variation in action and less repetition of questioning. The tension is well-maintained, but could be heightened with more proactive steps from the protagonist.
