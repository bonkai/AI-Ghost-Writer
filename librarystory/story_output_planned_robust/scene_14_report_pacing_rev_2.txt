*   **Escalation & Exit (Paragraphs 10-12):** The decision to leave is good, but the pacing here feels slightly rushed. The transition from realizing they’re being watched to actively escaping could benefit from a beat of internal panic or a brief struggle with the decision to abandon their research. The final image of Valerius watching them is strong, but could be even more impactful with a slightly slower reveal – perhaps Seris notices him *just* as they reach the archway, maximizing the feeling of being hunted.
