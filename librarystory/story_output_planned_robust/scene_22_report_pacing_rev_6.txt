*   **Initial Pacing:** The opening is solid, establishing urgency with Seris's physical reaction and Rhys's questioning. However, the rapid page-turning feels visually quick but doesn't translate to *felt* momentum. It's descriptive of action, not embodying it.
*   **Tension Build (Mid-Scene):** The fragmented passages and Kaelen’s projections are effective in building mystery. The reveal of “lost narratives” and “possibilities” is a good tension point. However, the dialogue feels slightly explanatory; showing more of Seris and Rhys *reacting* to the information would heighten tension.
*   **Peak & Valley – Illustration Reveal:** The illustration reveal is a strong peak. The rush of images and Seris stumbling is well-executed. However, the immediate aftermath – Seris clutching Rhys – feels a little drawn out before the crucial revelation about the Veil.
*   **Veil Revelation & Resolution:** The Veil revelation is a significant payoff. The final paragraph is strong, conveying the weight of their discovery and establishing a clear direction.
*   **Overall:** The scene is largely effective, but suffers from moments where it *tells* instead of *shows* the tension. Consider replacing some dialogue with internal reactions or physical manifestations of stress. The pacing is generally good, but could be punchier in the middle section.
