* **Dialogue & Pacing:** Reduce info-dumping in dialogue; weave exposition into action/reaction. Tighten pacing by streamlining the opening and adding more immediate threats.
* **Show, Don't Tell:** Strengthen character connection by *showing* trust building, not stating it. Replace some silent communication with nuanced reactions.
* **Tension Variety:** While atmospheric tension is strong, incorporate more physical action or near-misses to elevate the stakes and maintain momentum.
