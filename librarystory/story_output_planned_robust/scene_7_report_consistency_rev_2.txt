## Consistency Check: Passed.

This draft is remarkably consistent. There are no glaring plot holes or character inconsistencies. The progression of the vision and Seris's reaction feel natural and build effectively. Here's a breakdown of why it passes, with a few minor observations (not necessarily *inconsistencies*, just things to consider):

* **Established Mystery:** The initial setup of the sphere and the library is maintained throughout. The mystery deepens logically.
* **Seris's Reaction:** Her increasing disorientation and the way the vision affects her physically and emotionally are consistent. The feeling of "recognition" is introduced subtly and builds well.
* **Hemlock's Role:** Hemlock remains enigmatic and provides just enough information to fuel the mystery without revealing too much. His reactions and observations are consistent with his established character.
* **Vision Details:** The descriptions of the cities, the serpent, and the eye are vivid and consistent. The imagery is strong and reinforces the sense of ancient power.
* **Internal vs. External:** The blending of the vision with Seris's internal state (feeling the serpent's weariness, the "homecoming" sensation) is well-executed and consistent.
* **The 'Awakening' Theme:** The repeated use of "awakening" subtly reinforces the central mystery and suggests a larger, unfolding plot.



**Minor Observations (Not Inconsistencies):**

* **The Voice:** The voice being *felt* within her mind is a strong choice. It could be explored further - is this a common effect of the sphere, or is it specific to Seris?
* **Seris's Attunement:** Hemlock's comment about her being "more attuned" raises questions about her background. Is she descended from someone connected to these ancient cities? This is a good setup for future plot points.
* **The Serpent's Gaze:** The description of the serpent's gaze is powerful.  Consider if this gaze has a specific effect on Seris beyond simply overwhelming her. Does it impart knowledge, or alter her in some way?



**Overall:**

This is a strong piece of writing. The consistency is excellent, and the draft effectively builds suspense and mystery. The few minor observations are opportunities to deepen the story, but they do not represent inconsistencies.
