## Consistency Check Report - Draft 3 vs. Previous Drafts (Draft 1 & 2)

Here's a report detailing inconsistencies and a consistency check against the provided excerpts from Drafts 1 & 2. I'll categorize findings for clarity.

**Overall Assessment:** Draft 3 represents a significant shift in tone and focus. While not necessarily *inconsistent*, this represents a divergence from the earlier drafts' more direct threat.  The increased focus on psychological manipulation and the feeling of being a pawn is a key change.

* **The level of surveillance feels heightened in Draft 3.** The man in the coat, the silent figures in the courtyard, and the general sense of being watched are more pronounced than in earlier drafts.

The drafts are internally consistent, but Draft 3 represents a significant narrative shift in terms of the antagonist's strategy and the overall tone. The new elements (the man in the coat, the resonance with Firepeak) are not inconsistent but require further development to integrate seamlessly into the story.  The increased focus on psychological manipulation is a valid choice, but it changes the dynamic of the conflict.
