*   **“It remembers.”** – While atmospheric, this feels a bit *too* cryptic initially. It’s a strong line, but needs immediate follow-up to avoid feeling like vague pronouncements. Perhaps a beat of Seris reacting to it, prompting Kaelen to elaborate.
*   **Kaelen’s explanations are somewhat info-dumpy.** He delivers a lot of exposition about the Order, Firepeak, and Valerius relatively quickly. While necessary information, consider *showing* more of this through interaction or Seris’s deductions. For example, instead of "The Order didn’t just forbid magic…", perhaps Kaelen could react to something Seris says about magic, revealing the Order’s suppression as a response.
*   **Repetitive acknowledgement:** The stage direction “a shared acknowledgment of the danger” after the book slams shut feels redundant, given their prior dialogue and Kaelen’s tightening eyes. Trust the reader to infer their shared understanding.
*   **“Lost how?”** – This feels a little flat. Given the building tension and the weight of the situation, Seris's question could be more layered—perhaps hinting at a fear or a specific concern.
*   **Silent communication is overused.** While effective initially, the multiple instances of “silent message passing” and “didn’t need to speak” become noticeable. Consider replacing *some* of these with subtle physical reactions or brief, pointed dialogue.
*   **“We have to be careful.”** – This is a very generic line. Given the stakes, and the connection that’s supposedly sparking between them, this feels like a missed opportunity for a more emotionally resonant statement. Perhaps a line that acknowledges the personal risk, or a shared commitment to uncovering the truth.
*   **The connection between Seris and Kaelen feels rushed.** The "fragile trust" is stated rather than earned through dialogue. Show them building that trust through vulnerability, shared risk, or revealing personal details.

Overall, the dialogue isn’t *bad*, but it leans towards telling rather than showing. There's a lot of potential to deepen the emotional connection between the characters and create more suspense through subtext and carefully paced reveals.
